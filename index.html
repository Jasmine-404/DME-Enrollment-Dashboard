DME Enrollment Dashboard

This dashboard predicts enrollment for each grade in each facility in the DC Public School System from SY24-25 to SY29-30.
These predictions can be adjusted based on user-provided counts of construction permits issued in the school boundary, used as a proxy variable indicating the level of development around the school, and a change in capacity that impacts enrollment.
The predictions are made through regularized regression, validated on a year-by-year basis.
The DME Enrollment Dashboard was created as part of the semester-long practicum project for the Urban Spatial Analytics and City Planning programs at Penn's Weitzman School.
Project created by Jingmiao Fei, Jia Yue Ong, Amy Solano, and Neve Zhang.

    Variable Explanation

    Cumulative construction permits in 5 years: The total number of construction permits issued within the five years leading up to the selected school year. For example, if you're predicting for SY26-27, it includes all permits from 2021 to 2025.
    Change in school capacity 5 years later: The percentage change in a school's capacity over a five-year period. For example, if you're predicting for SY26-27, it compares capacity in SY26-27 to that in SY21-22.
